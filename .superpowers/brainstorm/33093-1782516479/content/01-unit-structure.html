First fork in the road: who are the "pieces"?

Chess emergence comes from multiple heterogeneous pieces interacting. The single biggest question that shapes everything else: in a fight, how many units does the player control?

A

Solo hero (or hero + small entourage)

Emergence lives in your kit: sequencing attacks across cooldowns, positioning one body against many, chaining status effects. The "multi-step plan" is a combo you set up over several turns.

B

Party / squad (Baldur's Gate-style)

Emergence lives between units: covering each other, forks, body-blocking lines of attack, baiting — the most literal chess analog. Each unit is a "piece" with its own weapons.

You can also tell me it's a hybrid (e.g. one main hero + 1–2 summons/companions). Just describe it in the terminal.
